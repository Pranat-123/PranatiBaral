THE PROCESS

How we work & What We do

DESIGN AND ROADMAP

We provided a structured framework for review, refinement and approval of content materials, fostering alignment with strategic objectives, maintaining high-quality standards and enhancing engagement through collaborative GS content development process.

Content kick-off

I discussed about objectives, timelines and established a clear content overview to ensure alignment and clarity. I analyzed the offering content visuals to make sure all in place and ready to start.

Fill in gaps

Based on the need, I discussed about additional relevant information or materials to address identified gaps to ensure completeness and relevance.

Design workshop and estimation

I collaborate and engage in workshop, discussion to refine content design and identify potential gaps, current ways of working, team insights with challenges and explore to identify solution. We validate the assigned task from monday board by complexity with length and follow the T-Shirt size estimation process.

Design guidelines

We follow the GS Design guidelines and standard for the template curation process. I built the template by using Master Layout Template to demonstrate various content layout options.

Review process

To follow self review process when my deck is ready, I go for self-review process and before that I make sure all the check list is place to verify. In self-review process I cross check the deck naming convention is right, slide sequence are right, I have transferred all necessary content and redesigned it, I have checked spelling and grammar, I have used high quality images, I have used only approved pictograms and images from IBM DAM and Pictogram library, I have checked all the hyperlinks and embedded ones that are needed - now links WORK in my deck as well, I used the design layouts from master layouts, I used approved fonts and style, the shapes are consistent and aligned + I have used the correct colors + I didn't insert additional text boxes within shapes, text formatting is correct, I have consistently used my layouts and used THE best layout that fits my content, performed sanitisation checklist and etc.

I also performed the role as "Peer Reviewer" and participated in Peer review process and one of the coach for design team and helped team as point of contact in offshore.

Testing and Sign-off

When the design and content revision are complete, and content gaps have been filled and we upload the design deck and update the status for "Ready for testing". Next step We perform the testing in "Qvdian" and take off for final sign-off and indicating approval for final design upload in system.

Qvdian" is a cloud-based automated proposal and sales document generation software, designed to streamline the creation of complex documents like proposals and RFPs (Request for Proposals).
