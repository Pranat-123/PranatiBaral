THE PROCESS

Design References

Throughout the entire design process, we built relationship and contributed solutions through various design forum engagements with client and other associated partners.As a UX designer, we did usability testing to see how gradual changes to the UI can affect user experience.

We ensured that client’s design frameworks are usable, accessible, and fit for purpose. Through the entire migration process, the digital toolkits and library are shared among our design team/dev team for migration from Angular to React.. Our thoughts and client’s feedback allows us to collaboratively shape our design identity, building toward a shared goal of creating enjoyable, memorable cross-platform experiences for all customers.

We designers understand the client’s design language system, UI toolkit and the wireframe design details. The toolkit library components includes both responsive and compact desktop, such as links, models, notifications, paginations, pills, progress indicator, progress tracker, radio buttons, section titles section tiles etc.

Migration and Implementation

We perform the accessibility testing in various environment in responsive way and raised bug tickets with issue summary details for related epics and assign them to respective scrum/squad leads. In next phase, when the design bugs were developed/fixed by the dev team they were sent across to functional QA for SIT, and once the functional QA team cleared them they sent across to UX testing design team for the UX sign off, before the PO finally closed the story. Also followed up with the Squrd/Scrum leds, Stakeholders day to day bug related queries and blockers based on need.
